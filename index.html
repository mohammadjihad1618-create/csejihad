Complete Cyber Security Guide (1 Year Roadmap)

🟢 What is Hacking?

Hacking means identifying weaknesses in systems, networks, or applications. It can be legal or illegal depending on intention.

⚪ White Hat Hacker

White hat hackers are ethical hackers. They work legally to find and fix security vulnerabilities. Companies hire them to protect systems.

⚫ Black Hat Hacker

Black hat hackers are illegal hackers. They break into systems to steal data, money, or cause damage.

⚙️ Grey Hat Hacker

Grey hat hackers are in between. They may hack without permission but usually don’t have harmful intentions.

📅 1 Year Learning Roadmap

Month 1-2: - Basic Computer Knowledge - Internet & Networking Basics - Learn Linux (Kali Linux)

Month 3-4: - HTML, CSS, JavaScript basics - Understand how websites work

Month 5-6: - Networking deep (IP, DNS, HTTP) - Tools: Nmap, Wireshark

Month 7-8: - Web Security Basics - Learn about XSS, SQL Injection (theory only)

Month 9-10: - Practice on legal platforms (CTF, labs) - Bug bounty basics

Month 11-12: - Advanced topics - Build your own tools - Start freelancing or internships

⚠️ Important Rule

Always use your skills legally. Never hack without permission.
