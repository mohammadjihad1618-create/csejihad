What is Ethical Hacking?

Ethical Hacking is the legal process of testing systems to find security vulnerabilities before malicious attackers exploit them. It helps companies protect data, networks and digital infrastructure.

Types of Hackers

🟢 White Hat Hacker: Legal security expert who protects systems.

⚪ Grey Hat Hacker: Finds vulnerabilities without permission but does not misuse them.

🔴 Black Hat Hacker: Illegal attacker who breaks into systems for personal gain.
