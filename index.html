What is Ethical Hacking?

Ethical hacking is the legal practice of testing systems and networks to find security vulnerabilities before criminals exploit them. It is always done with permission.

Types of Hackers

White Hat Hacker

White hat hackers are legal security professionals who protect companies from cyber attacks. They perform penetration testing and improve system security.

Grey Hat Hacker

Grey hat hackers operate between legal and illegal boundaries. They may find vulnerabilities without permission but usually do not have malicious intent.

Black Hat Hacker

Black hat hackers are criminals who steal data, spread malware, and perform illegal cyber attacks for money or damage.
